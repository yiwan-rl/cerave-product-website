About CeraVe Daily Moisturizing Lotion

CeraVe Daily Moisturizing Lotion is a lightweight, oil-free moisturizer that helps hydrate the skin and restore its natural barrier. Developed with dermatologists, this lotion is suitable for dry skin, sensitive skin, and acne-prone skin. It contains hyaluronic acid and ceramides to provide long-lasting hydration and help maintain the skin's natural moisture barrier.

CeraVe Daily Moisturizing Lotion is a smooth, light-weight moisturizing lotion for normal to dry skin. It is non-comedogenic and has a smooth, light-weight texture that is absorbed quickly, leaving skin feeling smooth and hydrated.
