Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I buy CeraVe products?

CeraVe products are available at most major retailers, drugstores, and online marketplaces. You can also purchase directly from our website for exclusive offers and promotions.

Are CeraVe products suitable for sensitive skin?

Yes, CeraVe products are formulated with sensitive skin in mind. They are fragrance-free, non-comedogenic, and contain essential ceramides that help restore and maintain the skin's natural barrier.

What is the return policy?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on all products purchased directly from our website. If you're not completely satisfied, you can return the product for a full refund.

Are CeraVe products tested on animals?

CeraVe does not test our products on animals and we do not ask others to test on our behalf. We are committed to ending animal testing across our industry.
